Overview

I have submitted my Ph.D. thesis under the guidance of Prof. Sundar Vishwanathan in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at IIT Bombay. My research interests include extremal combinatorics, graph theory, probabilistic problems on graphs and hypergraphs and applications in theoretical computer science.

Prior to my Ph.D. I was a Junior Research Fellow at Institute of Mathematical Science, Chennai (IMSc. Chennai). I did my M.Tech from IIT Kanpur. My Master's Thesis was under the guidance of Prof. Somenath Biswas, Prof.Nisheeth Vishnoi and Prof. Piyush Kurur. I have spent a brief time as a visiting student at Microsoft Research, Bangalore (MSR India).
